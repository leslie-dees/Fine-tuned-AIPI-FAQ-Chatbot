AIPI 520: Modeling Process & Algorithms Course Syllabus

Learning Objectives

At the end of this course, students will be able to:

Apply best practices to perform the end-to-end process of training machine learning models
Explain the strengths, weaknesses, assumptions, mathematical theory and use cases for the major types of supervised and unsupervised machine learning algorithms
Consider the implications of the bias-variance tradeoff in performing the modeling process
Evaluate and interpret model performance

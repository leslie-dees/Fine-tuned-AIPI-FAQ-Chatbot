MENG 570: Business Fundamentals for Engineers Course Syllabus

The learning objectives of this course are:
? Speak the language of business.
? Understand the full cycle of business, what the primary business functions do, and the importance of innovation
in business. 
? Gain experience creating a lean business plan from scratch, developing an understanding of the key success
factors for a new, differentiated business (whether as part of an independent start-up or a seasoned company).
? Practice interpreting and using financial data -- income statement, balance sheet, cash flow statement.
? Practice making decisions based on business data, albeit imperfect data.
? Think and plan strategically. Specifically, create a strategy and make business decisions in alignment with the
strategy.
? Operate in a complex and somewhat ambiguous business environment.
? Practice emotional intelligence, leadership, teamwork and presentation skills
